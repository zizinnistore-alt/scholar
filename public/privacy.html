Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

Scholar Nexus collects minimal personal data necessary to provide our services. When you use our job application feature, we temporarily process your CV/Resume to forward it to the respective employers.

2. AI & Data Processing

When using the "Topic Explorer" or "Scanner", the text you input is sent securely to our AI providers (Google Gemini) and academic partners (Semantic Scholar). Please do not submit confidential or unreleased proprietary research data into the public scanners.

3. Third-Party Links

Our platform indexes global academic profiles and jobs. Clicking "View Profile" or "Apply" may direct you to third-party institutions or Semantic Scholar pages which have their own independent privacy policies.

4. Your Rights

If you are a researcher listed in our database and wish to update your AI summary or opt-out of indexing, please contact us. Note that our baseline data is derived from public academic graphs.
